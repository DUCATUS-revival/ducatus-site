About DucatusX

DucatusX is a fork of the Ethereum blockchain, therefore it supports both transactions and creations of smart contracts. Unlike Ethereum, DucatusX uses a “Proof of Authority” consensus algorithm which reduces the maintenance cost. In the future, it is considered to switch to “Proof of Stake” which may give users even more confidence in choosing DucatusX for their projects. The blocks are produced regularly by 3 proven secured nodes resulting in stable cost and speed of block production.
